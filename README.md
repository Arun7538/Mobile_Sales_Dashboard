# 📱 Mobile Sales Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
The **Mobile Sales Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze mobile phone sales performance across different cities, brands, payment methods, and time periods. The dashboard provides meaningful insights into sales trends, customer transactions, product performance, and rating analysis, helping businesses make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
* Analyze overall mobile sales performance.
* Track sales trends across different months.
* Compare sales among various mobile brands and models.
* Monitor transaction patterns based on payment methods.
* Evaluate customer ratings and satisfaction levels.
* Identify top-performing cities and products.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1. Sales Overview
* Total Sales: **769M**
* Total Quantity Sold: **19K**
* Total Transactions: **4K**
* Average Sales Value: **40.11K**

### 2. City-wise Sales Analysis
* Interactive map visualization.
* Displays sales distribution across major Indian cities.
* Helps identify high-performing locations.

### 3. Monthly Sales Trend
* Line chart showing quantity sold month-wise.
* Enables seasonal trend analysis.

### 4. Brand Performance Analysis
* Comparison of sales and transactions across different brands.
* Helps identify leading mobile manufacturers.

### 5. Mobile Model Analysis
* Highlights top-selling mobile models.
* Compares revenue generated by different devices.

### 6. Payment Method Analysis
* Distribution of transactions by:

  * UPI
  * Debit Card
  * Credit Card
  * Cash

### 7. Customer Rating Analysis
* Categorizes customer feedback into:

  * Good
  * Average
  * Poor
* Measures customer satisfaction levels.

### 8. Day-wise Sales Analysis
* Tracks sales performance across weekdays.
* Helps identify peak business days.
